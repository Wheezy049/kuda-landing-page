Save

Putting money away for the future is smart but it
takes discipline - something humans don’t have in
abundance. We’re using technology to solve that by
automating saving. You set an amount to save and
the Kuda app takes care of the rest, prompt interest
payout included.

Spend

Kuda gives you more than one way to pay easily,
including a widely accepted debit card issued in
partnership with Visa, quick web payments direct
from your Kuda account, and location-based gift
cards for shopping and subscriptions.

Send

From international transfers to on-the-spot payments
at market stalls, we’ve simplified sending money so
you don’t need to think too much about it.

You can find your friends on Kuda and make unlimited
instant transfers free of charge, send money to local
banks without breaking a sweat, or fulfil your
promises to family across borders.

Invest

We’re bringing verified global investment options
closer to every African with fractional shares on Kuda,
giving anyone with a Kuda account the opportunity to
grow their money wherever they are in the world.

We’re always happy to help you.

You can chat with us on the app, slide into our
DMs, tweet, leave an Instagram comment, send
an email or call. However you choose to reach
out, there’ll always be a friendly person there to
make your life easy.
